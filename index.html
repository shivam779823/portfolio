About Me

Get to know me more

It all started with a simple fascination for technology and a drive to solve problems. Little did I know, that spark would guide me into a fulfilling career as a DevOps Engineer. With 2.8 years of experience under my belt, I’ve had the privilege of working at companies like Deutsche Bank and Cognizant, where I’ve tackled everything from automating processes to managing complex cloud infrastructures. Early on, I found myself drawn to the challenges of streamlining workflows. One of my proudest moments was leading the automation of CI/CD pipelines and implementing Infrastructure as Code with Terraform. These experiences not only strengthened my technical skills but also deepened my passion for making systems more efficient and scalable. Working on GCP, I fine-tuned cloud configurations, ensuring that everything ran like a well-oiled machine. Each project I undertook pushed me to learn and grow, earning me multiple Google Cloud skill badges and certifications along the way. Today, I thrive on making meaningful changes that optimize development processes and cloud systems. And while I’m proud of what I’ve accomplished, I know there’s always more to learn, more to improve, and more impact to be made. That’s what keeps me going.
